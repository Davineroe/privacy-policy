Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

XML Media Player ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Data Collection

XML Media Player does not collect personal data from users.

The app may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Playlist URLs or imported M3U/M3U8 files
  • Xtream login information (host, username, password)
  • Favorites and playback history
  • App settings and preferences

All data is stored locally and is not transmitted to external servers.

2. Use of Data

The stored data is used only to:

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Load and manage your playlists
  • Improve user experience
  • Save your preferences and playback history

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use:

  • Apple App Store for distribution and in-app purchases

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

4. Content Responsibility

XML Media Player does not provide any media content.

Users are responsible for the content they access through the app and must ensure they have the legal rights to use their playlists or streaming services.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device and protected by Apple’s iOS security system.

Deleting the app will permanently remove all stored data.

6. User Rights

  • You can delete your data at any time within the app
  • You can remove all data by uninstalling the app

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
