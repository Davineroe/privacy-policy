Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection

The AnyWorld application collects only the minimum data necessary for proper functionality:

  • Favorites / Watchlist: Anime and shows you save are stored locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences are stored locally to improve your experience.
  • Quiz or Progress Data: If available, your progress is stored locally on your device.

AnyWorld does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or account credentials.

2. Use of Data

The data stored by the application is used exclusively to:

  • Provide a personalized anime and shows discovery experience.
  • Save your favorites and watchlist.
  • Maintain your app preferences.
  • Improve the user experience within the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services, including:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates.
  • Third-party content APIs: To provide anime/show information, images, descriptions, and metadata.
  • YouTube or external platforms: To display official trailers when available.
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements, if enabled in the app.

These third-party services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Advertising

AnyWorld may display advertisements using Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect limited information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, and interaction data to provide and improve ads.

You can limit ad tracking from your device settings.

5. Storage and Security

All user data such as favorites, watchlist, and preferences is stored locally on your device.

AnyWorld does not store personal data on external servers.

Deleting the app will permanently remove all associated local data from your device.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and manage your saved data inside the app.
  • Delete your saved data by removing it from the app.
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the application.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions.

7. Copyright and Content Disclaimer

AnyWorld is an anime and shows discovery application.

The app does not host, stream, or distribute full copyrighted anime episodes, movies, or shows.

All information, images, descriptions, ratings, and trailers are provided by third-party sources and remain the property of their respective owners.

AnyWorld is intended for informational and entertainment purposes only.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the application, legal requirements, or third-party services.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
