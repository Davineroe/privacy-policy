Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection

The NeonFlix application collects only the minimum data necessary for proper functionality:

  • Favorites (Watchlist): Movies and shows you save are stored locally on your device.
  • Quiz Progress: Your quiz scores and progress are saved locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Settings and preferences are stored locally to improve your experience.

No personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, or account credentials is collected or stored by NeonFlix.

2. Use of Data

The data stored by the application is used exclusively to:

  • Provide a personalized movie and TV show discovery experience.
  • Save your favorites and watchlist for easy access.
  • Track your quiz performance and progress.
  • Maintain your preferences for a smoother user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

The application may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and updates.
  • TMDb API: To provide movie and TV show information, metadata, and images.
  • YouTube: To display official trailers when available.

NeonFlix does not use third-party advertising networks and does not include external analytics services unless stated otherwise in a future update.

4. Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device and protected by the security mechanisms provided by Apple and iOS.

No data is transmitted to or stored on external NeonFlix servers.

Deleting the application will permanently remove all associated local data from your device.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and manage your saved data directly within the app.
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions or concerns.

6. Copyright and Content Disclaimer

NeonFlix is a movie and TV show discovery application. It does not host, stream, or distribute full copyrighted movies or TV shows.

All movie and TV show data, descriptions, ratings, and images are provided by third-party sources such as TMDb, subject to their respective terms and licenses.

Trailers displayed in the app are official trailers made publicly available through platforms such as YouTube.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the application, legal requirements, or third-party services.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
